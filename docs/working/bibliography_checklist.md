# Bibliography Checklist: Rapa Nui Chiefdom Attribution

Master chronological checklist of all sources bearing on the Rapa Nui chiefdom question. Covers the full timeline from first European contact (1722) through current research (2025). Tracks acquisition status and pipeline progress for each source.

**Pipeline stages**: not started → claims → summary → evidence → complete
**Priority levels**: critical (core to the argument) / important (significantly supports or qualifies) / supplementary (useful context)

Last updated: 2026-03-16
